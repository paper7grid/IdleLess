Screen Time & Mental Health Project

Welcome to our project exploring the relationship between digital screen usage and mental wellbeing. We aim to understand how screen time and sleep impact stress and mood through data analysis and interactive visualizations.

The Problem

In today's digital world, many people spend excessive hours on their phones and screens, which has been linked to increased stress levels and mood disorders. The challenge is to understand these connections clearly so individuals can make informed decisions about their screen habits and prioritize mental health.

The Solution

We analyzed real-world data about daily screen time, sleep hours, stress levels, and mood scores to uncover meaningful patterns. Our Streamlit website offers an interactive experience where users can input their own data and see personalized insights, along with visual summaries of larger trends.
